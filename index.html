Me in a Nutshell

I am a final-year PhD candidate in the Department of Computing at Imperial College London, supervised by Dr. Sergio Maffeis. My research focuses on improving deep learning-based network intrusion detection systems (NIDS). Specifically, I am trying to:


  • Adapt one-class anomaly detection NIDS to handle changes in normal (benign) behavior.
  • Enable multi-class NIDS to adapt to new attacks as they emerge over time.
  • Enhance the robustness of these systems against noisy labels and label-flipping attacks.

By adapt, I mean developing systems that can detect and adjust to changes with minimal cost.
